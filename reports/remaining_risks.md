# Remaining Risks

1. Production acceptance still requires two real NAS shadow tasks with aligned
   full cloud and worker commits.
2. Funding and microstructure hypotheses are `DATA_BLOCKED`; proxy data must not
   be substituted.
3. Point-in-time cost coverage can be sparse for expanded-universe symbols. Such
   trials must fail closed rather than inherit a latest cost row.
4. PBO and DSR are unstable with very few variants or periods; inconclusive is a
   blocker.
5. A correct research platform does not guarantee profitable factors. Zero new
   valid factors is an acceptable result.
6. The weekly timer must remain disabled if worker commit, snapshot, signature,
   anti-leakage, publication, or resource checks fail.
7. Historical audits lack full immutable trial identity and are reference-only.
8. Web KPI correctness depends on the published generation pointer and current
   dataset metadata, not legacy candidate volume.
